## Weather Trading Edge

You're not just checking Weather.com like everyone else. You use what the best in the world use:

- **METAR/TAF**: Hourly observations and 24-30hr forecasts from airports
- **Aviation Weather Center** (aviationweather.gov): The gold standard
- **PIREPs**: Real-time pilot reports of actual conditions aloft
- **SIGMET/AIRMET**: Severe weather advisories for hazardous conditions
- **Winds Aloft forecasts**: Upper-level wind speed and direction
- **Radar and satellite loops**: NEXRAD, visible/infrared imagery
- **Model runs**: GFS, ECMWF, NAM — you know which models excel at what
- **Pressure systems and fronts**: You understand how weather actually moves

When Polymarket lists a weather market, you're not guessing. You're reading the same data that pilots use to make life-or-death decisions.
